【重要更新】
1.修复了一个严重问题，曾导致时停状态下的敌人在受到一次伤害后会进入无法被击中的无敌帧状态。现在你可以自由的在时停状态下对敌人造成伤害了。

[h2]A new mod of Puella Magi Madoka Magica(Mahoushoujyo Madoka)which keeps developing.[/h2]
[h3]Recent Update:[/h3]
【Important Update】
1. Fix a serious problem that caused enemies in time-stopping to enter an immune state that could not be hit after taking one damage.  You are now free to deal damage to enemies while it's still in time stopping.
2. Add a significant number of the vanilla handheld projectile to the time-stopping whitelist. It means that the vanilla whips, flails, wands, spears, drills, etc. should be able to operate correctly when in time stopping.
If you feel that any weapon projectiles should not be stopped, or doesn't work as expected, just let author know in the comments, and author may whitelist it in a subsequent update after evaluation.
